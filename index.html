Appetizer

Crispy Kimchi Pancake

A kimchi pancake is one of the easiest and quickest things to make and is one of Koreans’ all time favorite dishes. It’s hot, spicy, crispy, a little sweet, sour, and a little salty. It is not only a great snack but also it goes well with rice as a side dish.

Dessert

Yakgwa 약과 (Honey Pastry)

Yakgwa is a traditional Korean sweet, and it's usually shaped into some sort of flower design. It's deep-fried and very sweet, so it's more of a dessert than an everyday cookie, and it was traditionally served at ceremonies, celebrations, and on special occasions.

Vegan

Japchae 잡채 (Glass noodle stir fry)

Japchae is a sweet and savory dish of stir-fried glass noodles and vegetables that is popular in Korean cuisine.

Dessert

Bingsu 빙수 (Korean ice cream)

Bingsu is a popular Korean shaved ice dessert with sweet toppings that may include chopped fruit, condensed milk, fruit syrup, and red beans.

Appetizer

Mandu 만두 (Korean Dumplings)

Mandu are dumplings in Korean cuisine. Mandu can be steamed, boiled, pan-fried, or deep-fried. Mandu are traditionally made with a beef or pork filling, but chicken and vegetarian dumplings are also popular.
