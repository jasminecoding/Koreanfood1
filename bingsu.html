Strawberry Bingsu Recipe

4.0 from 20 reviews
  • Cook time: 3 hours
  • Yield: 1 - 3 Servings
Print Recipe
Ingredients
  • 4 strawberries (plus extra for serving)
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1½ cups milk
  • Ice cream
  • Whipped cream
  • Condensed milk

Nutrition Facts
Serves 3
  • Total Fat : 15g
  • Sugars : 35g
  • Calories: 200

Description

Bingsu is a popular Korean shaved ice dessert with sweet toppings that may include chopped fruit, condensed milk, fruit syrup, and red beans.

Instructions

  • 01. Dice strawberries and mash with a fork in a bowl until it becomes a lumpy puree.
  • 02. Add in the sugar and mix well. Then, add in the milk and mix well.
  • 03. With a large zip lock bag, pour in the mixture and release as much air as possible.
  • 04. Seal and lay flat in the freezer for 2½-3 hrs.
  • 05. Cut extra strawberries in half, and reserve 1 whole strawberry for the top. Set aside.
  • 06. Remove the mixture from the freezer and bash with a rolling pin.
  • 07. Place in a dessert bowl and lay the halved strawberries around the bingsu and the whole strawberry on top.
  • 08. Add ice cream, whipped cream and/or drizzle condensed milk to your liking. Serve immediately.

Notes

i

You can choose any flavor of fruit you want to your liking.
