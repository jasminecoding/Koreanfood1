Boba Recipe

4.5 from 74 reviews
  • Prep time: 5 min
  • Cook time: 15 min
  • Yield: 2
Print Recipe
Ingredients
    Black tea:
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 2 tablepsoons black tea

    • Dark brown syrup:
  • 1/4 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup water

    • Tapioca balls:
  • 4 cups water
  • 3/4 cup tapioca balls

    • Cream froth:
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • Ice
Nutrition Facts
Serves 2
  • Total Fat : 4g
  • Sugars : 38g
  • Calories: 200

Description

Decadent, delicious bubble tea drink made with tapioca balls, dark brown sugar, and milk tea.

Instructions

  • Make tea:

    01. Boil 2 3/4 cup of water. If using an electric kettle with a temperature setting, set the water to 208Â°F. (Use filtered water if possible.)
  • 02. Combine tea and hot water in a teapot. Use 2 tablespoons of black tea in 2 1/2 cups of water. (We're saving the leftover 1/4 cup of hot water for the brown sugar simple syrup.)
  • 03. Cover teapot and steep for 5 minutes.
  • 04. Strain tea leaves and let tea cool.
    • Make brown sugar syrup:

  • 05. Stir together hot water and dark brown sugar until sugar dissolves. Take the 1/4 cup hot water you have left over and stir in 1/4 cup dark brown sugar.
    • Make tapioca balls:

  • 06. Boil 4 cups of water on the stovetop then add tapioca balls. In a couple of minutes, they'll start floating to the surface. Cover the pot and cook on medium heat for 15 minutes. Stir occasionally.
  • 07. Strain tapioca balls.
  • 08. Take the dark brown simple syrup we made earlier and pour it into the pot with the boba. Stir to combine. Let cool slightly.
    • Make cream froth:

  • 09. Lightly whip heavy cream and sugar. Cream froth is a lightly whipped heavy cream. Use a handheld milk frother and whip for 10 seconds.
    • Assemble bubble tea:

  • 10. Divide each component into two cups. Spoon in boba in brown sugar simple syrup, add ice, then black tea, and top with cream froth. Stir together before drinking.

Notes

i

Make sure your tapioca balls are still nice and warm when you assemble the drink. They'll be nice and soft with a little chew.

i

Instead of loose tea, you can use 3 tea sachets or 6 tea bags.

i

Use an extra wide straw to drink your bubble tea.
